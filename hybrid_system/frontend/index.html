📝 Note for Evaluators: Modality Upgrade
The underlying algorithm mapping this survey to baseline stress was recently upgraded from a flat Logistic Regression pipeline to a Random Forest Classifier. Since subjective human psychometrics (like a 1-5 scale) contain high non-linear variability, Random Forests capture feature interactions vastly better—ensuring higher prediction validation before the camera even turns on.
🔍 How the Live Camera Predicts Emotions & Stress:
The OpenCV camera uses geometric math on your 478 face dots, not a black-box AI. Here are the exact factors and stress weights:
  • Angry (Stress=5): Triggered by extreme brow drops, tightened lips, and narrowed eyes. We've weighted this strictly so resting faces aren't flagged as angry.
  • Sad/Disgusted (Stress=4): Triggered by frowning (lip corners down) or asymmetrical lip tension.
  • Surprised (Stress=3): Triggered by raised brows + wide eyes.
  • Neutral (Stress=2): The default resting face. If no expression scores above a 55% confidence threshold, the system defaults to Neutral.
  • Happy (Stress=1): Triggered actively by smiling (lip corners curling upwards).
